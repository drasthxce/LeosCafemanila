Leo's Cafe

About us



Leo’s Café, a coffee shop located in 1198 Asuncion St. Tondo, Manila, Manila. The café was established on August 9, 2018, and offers a variety of hot and cold beverages, including coffee and smoothies, as well as some food items such as burgers, carbonara, and desserts. The owner of the café is Sir Leo Richmond Arguelles. A product that sir Leo recommends is the salted caramel latte, which is known for its unique sweet and salty taste.
